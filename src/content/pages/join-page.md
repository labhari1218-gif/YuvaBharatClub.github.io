Yuva Bharat Club grows through students who are willing to participate with care, responsibility, and initiative. You do not need to fit a single mold to contribute meaningfully.

**जो छात्र सीखते हुए काम करना चाहते हैं, उनके लिए भी जगह है।**

## Ways to contribute

- support event planning and on-ground coordination
- help with moderation, anchoring, or public speaking formats
- design posters, social assets, and announcement material
- document activities through notes, photography, and reports
- assist with outreach and volunteer coordination

## What to expect

YBC works best when contributors are consistent, respectful, and ready to collaborate across teams. Most activities involve shared ownership rather than isolated tasks.

## Before joining

Use only approved public forms, email addresses, and media. Private member data, personal numbers, and informal coordination details should never be published through the public site.
